# Yuelu Tech Limited - Open Source Initiatives 🌍

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **[YUELU TECH LIMITED](https://yuelu.tech)**. 

Headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda, we are an AI company focused on deploying practical, production-grade intelligence in emerging markets. We bridge the technological capabilities of Yuelu (Changsha, China) with the real-world agricultural needs of East Africa.

## 🚀 Our Focus: AI for Agriculture

Our core product is a multilingual agricultural assistant delivered via WhatsApp, designed for environments with low connectivity. We utilize a robust **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) + LLM** architecture to ensure our AI does not hallucinate, but rather grounds its advice in verified local agronomic data.

### Repositories Overview
In this organization, we will periodically open-source non-confidential datasets and tools that assist in our mission, including:
- Multilingual NLP mappings (Kinyarwanda, French, English, Swahili)
- RAG prompt structures for precision agricultural advisory
- Lightweight API connection templates for WhatsApp Business
